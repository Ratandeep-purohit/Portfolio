My Engineering Philosophy

The principles that guide every line of code I write.

Clean Architecture First

I don't just write scripts; I engineer systems. Whether it's a FastAPI microservice or a Django monolith, I enforce strict separation of concerns, DRY principles, and modular design to ensure the codebase can scale from 10 to 10,000 users without breaking a sweat.

Data-Driven AI

AI is only as good as its data. I focus on feature engineering, robust vector search (Qdrant), and model interpretability (SHAP) rather than just blind API wrappers.

Obsessive Optimization

From O(1) database queries to lazy-loading React components, I am obsessed with squeezing out every millisecond of performance.

Security by Default

With a deep interest in Cyber Security, I build applications assuming they are under attack. Implementing JWT auth, role-based access control (RBAC), and SQL injection prevention is standard procedure, not an afterthought.
